About Us

Our Company

We are a company that makes and distributes gluten-free snacks. Our main product is made with love and served in 5-star restaurants across the nation. Our target audience is couples. We want to convey a sense of innocence, while at the same time being gentle.

Our Background

Gluten is generally used to give a chewy and elastic texture to food dough. Although gluten is classified as safe for consumption, it can also be harmful to health if consumed in excess. The problem with consuming too much wheat flour is that it can cause celiac disease. Celiac disease is an autoimmune disease that occurs as a result of consuming gluten. Therefore, we are here to help reduce gluten consumption by making gluten-free products.
